About Us

We are a company that specializes in the sale of Renewable Energy Credits (RECs) to businesses and individuals who want to support clean energy.

RECs represent the environmental benefits of renewable energy generation, and by purchasing RECs, you can help reduce greenhouse gas emissions and support the development of renewable energy projects.

Our platform is designed to be user-friendly and accessible, allowing businesses of all sizes to participate in the renewable energy market. Our platform will allow users to buy and sell RECs with ease, providing a transparent and secure way to trade RECs. The platform will also feature tools to help users understand the value of RECs and make informed decisions about their investments.

One of the key benefits of our platform is the ability to create a market for smaller-scale renewable energy producers. Currently, many smaller producers do not have access to the same markets as larger producers, making it difficult for them to sell their RECs. Our platform will create a more level playing field, allowing smaller producers to compete and contribute to the renewable energy market.

Finally, our platform will help to accelerate the transition to renewable energy by making it easier and more cost-effective for businesses to invest in renewable energy. By streamlining the process of buying and selling RECs, we will create a more efficient market for renewable energy production, leading to increased investment and adoption of renewable energy sources.

Our Services

We offer a range of services to help businesses and individuals purchase and sell RECs:

REC Purchase: We can help you purchase RECs from renewable energy projects across the country.

REC Sales: If you have excess RECs that you want to sell, we can help you find buyers and get the best price.

REC Brokerage: We act as a broker between buyers and sellers to facilitate REC transactions.
