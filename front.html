About Us

We are a company that specializes in the sale of Renewable Energy Credits (RECs) to businesses and individuals who want to support clean energy.

RECs represent the environmental benefits of renewable energy generation, and by purchasing RECs, you can help reduce greenhouse gas emissions and support the development of renewable energy projects.

Our Services

We offer a range of services to help businesses and individuals purchase and sell RECs:

  • REC Purchase: We can help you purchase RECs from renewable energy projects across the country.
  • REC Sales: If you have excess RECs that you want to sell, we can help you find buyers and get the best price.
  • REC Brokerage: We act as a broker between buyers and sellers to facilitate REC transactions.
